Caramel Bread Pudding

Caramel Bread Pudding is a melt in the mouth pudding made with caramel and bread slices. This pudding is prepared by steaming method and hence no need of an oven in this recipe. Steaming can be done in a pan/pressure cooker/an electric rice cooker. Dry fruits like cashews/raisins/almonds enhance the flavor of this pudding. This is a simple dessert to prepare when you have guests coming home or after a weeknight meal. Also it does not take up much time and can be prepared in a jiff. Make this Caramel Bread Pudding at your next get together as a Party Dessert and have everyone asking for more!

Orange Rasmalai

The Orange Rasmalai Recipe is a twist to the traditional recipe where the Rasmalais are flavored with oranges. Rasmalai is a very popular Indian dessert which is made using fresh chenna (cottage cheese). Small dumplings are made with this chenna which are cooked in a sugar syrup and then soaked in flavored, lightly condensed milk.
