Sichuan Dan-Dan Noodles

Sichuan Dan Dan Noodles Recipe (Sesame Noodles With Crispy Tofu Recipe) is a famous street food dish originating from Sichuan region of China. Traditionally, the noodles are tossed with "Sichuan Chili oil" and sesame or peanut butter to give a nutty flavor to the whole dish.Typically the noodles are topped with crispy fried minced pork with chili oil. The word "Dan Dan" refers to "carrying pole". In the past, the vendors carried the noodles and the sauces in these poles to sell them on the street.
