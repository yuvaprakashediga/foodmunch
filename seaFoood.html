Amritsari Fish Fry

Amritsar Fish Fry Recipe is a wonderful dry fish made with a spicy masala that is coated over the fish and shallow fried till it is roasted well. It is a popular street food that many people would love to try it out at their place. Here we have used seer fish to make this recipe but, you can also use any flat fish of your choice to bring out a different flavor, Fish is a very good source of protein and it is this is the best way to add it into your diet as it uses less oil to cook the fish.

Moroccan Baked Fish

Morocco is a North African country, bordering the North Atlantic Ocean and Mediterranean Sea. Spices like paprika, cumin and saffron are widely used in Moroccan food. A firm fleshed fish like salmon or basa fish is used to make this dish. Moroccan Baked Fish recipe is a popular dish made in Morocco and Mediterranean region. The fish is baked on a bed of spiced Moroccan sauce which is made using coriander leaves or parsley, onion, garlic, olives and various spices. This dish is served with a bread named Challah or Moroccan baked bread. Some people even serve it with vegetables like potatoes or asparagus and white rice.

Spicy Chettinad Fish Fry

Spicy Chettinad Fish Fry Recipe is a delicious and spicy preparation of fish made in the Chettinad region of South India. Chettinad cuisine is known for spicy food which is made using freshly ground masalas. You can use any type of firm fleshed fish to make this recipe. I used surmai/seerfish.
